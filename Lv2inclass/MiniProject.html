About The Basketball league NBA

The NBA consists of 30 Teams in the United States and Canada. These teams are divided into the Eastern Confrence and the Western Confrence. The NBA has a 82 game schedule begains in October to mid april. The league hold 'play in' tournements to determine the last two teams in both confrences. eight teams in the confrence qualify for the playoffs. The NBA sponsers the NBA-G league (NBA gatorade league) which is a minor league and provides an expirence for player, coaches and refs for a job in the NBA.
